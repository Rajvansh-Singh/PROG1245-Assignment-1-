Albert Einstein

Biography

Albert Einstein was a German-born theoretrical physicist who developed the Theory of Relativity , one of the two pillars of modern physics(the other being quantum mechanics). He is best known for his mass-energy equivalence formula E=mc2. Einstein's groundbreaking work revolutionized our understanding of the universe and led to numerous technological advancements.
